Fried Egg

Description of an egg recipe, according to Tony Blair

"What more really needs to be said? as traditional as it gets, whats more homely than the humble egg?" - Tony Blair

Ingredients!

  • An egg
  • Another egg
  • And another

Steps

  1. Heat up the fancy pan
  2. Crack the egg in
  3. Fry it til you cant fry no more
